Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Gen’s subscription plans and features.

What is included in each subscription plan?

The Basic plan (3 Pi/month) includes AI habit tracking, basic task management, community challenges, cross-platform sync, and Pi payments. The Pro plan (6 Pi/month) adds mood-based suggestions, advanced task prioritization, priority support, and custom challenge creation. The Premium plan (12 Pi/month) includes everything in Pro, plus personalized AI coaching, an advanced analytics dashboard, exclusive community events, and early access to new features.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle, and you’ll be charged the new plan’s rate.

How do Pi payments work?

Gen uses the Pi Network for secure, cryptocurrency-based payments. You’ll need a Pi wallet to pay with Pi. The Pi Browser handles transactions seamlessly, and prices are shown in Pi with approximate USD equivalents.

Is there a free trial?

Currently, Gen offers a 7-day free trial for Pro and Premium plans. You can try all features before committing to a paid subscription.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime via your account settings. You’ll retain access to your plan’s features until the end of the current billing period, after which you’ll revert to a free account with limited features.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for monthly plans and 30 days for yearly plans, provided you haven’t extensively used premium features. Contact support for assistance.
